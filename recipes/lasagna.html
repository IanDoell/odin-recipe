Lasagna


Recipe

Yield: 12 Servings - Prep: 60 min - Cook: 50 min

Ingredients

  • (9) Lasagna noodles
  • (3/4 pound) Ground beef
  • (1) Medium onion
  • (3) Garlic cloves
  • (2 cans) Crushed tomatoes
  • (2 cans) Tomato paste
  • (2/3 cup) Water
  • (2-3 tbsp) Sugar
  • (1 carton) Ricotta cheese
  • (4 cup) Shredded mozzarella
  • (3/4 cup) Grated Parmesan
  • (9 tbsp) Italian seasonings
  • (1) Egg

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  2. Cook beef and onion over medium heat 8-10 minutes. Add garlic, cook 1 minute.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, italian seasonings, and pinch of salt.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees farenheit. Spread 2 cups meat sauce into 13x9in baking dish.
  5. Layer with 3 noodles and 1/3 ricotta, sprinkle 1 cup mozarella and 2 tbsp parmesan. Repeat layers twice.
  6. Bake, covered, 25 minutes. Bake uncovered 25 minutes longer or until bubly. Let stand 15 mins.
