Nachos

Description

This take on nachos is extremely time friendly, and will leave you and your wallet satisfied!
Whip these up in under 20 minutes, and enjoy a weeknight comfort staple.

Recipe

Yield: 10 Servings - Prep: 10 min - Cook: 8 min

Ingredients

  • (10 oz) Torilla chips
  • (2 cup) Ground beef
  • (15 oz) Black beans
  • (1) Onion, chopped
  • (1 cup) Black olives, sliced
  • (1/4 cup) Jalapenos, sliced
  • (1 cup) Pepper jack, shredded
  • (1 cup) Mild cheddar, shredded
  • (1/2 cup) Sour cream
  • (1/2 cup) Guacamole
  • (1/2 cup) Salsa

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit.
  2. Spread tortilla chips on large cookie sheet, spread cooked beef over top.
  3. Spread black beans, olives, jalapenos, and cheeses over the top.
  4. Add one more layer and repeat steps.
  5. Put in oven for roughly 8 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
  6. Let stand for 2 mins after removing from oven. Serve with condiments on the side.
