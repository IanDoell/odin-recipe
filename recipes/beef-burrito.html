Beef Burrito

Recipe

Yield: 6 burritos - Prep: 25 min - Cook: 20 min

Ingredients

  • (1 pound) Lean ground beef
  • (1) Small yellow onion
  • (1) Taco seasoning packet
  • (2 tbsp) Chopped fresh cilantro
  • (6) Large flour tortillas
  • (1 can) Refried beans
  • (1.5 cup) Shredded cheese

Instructions

  1. In a large skiller over medium-high heat add ground beef and onion.
  2. Cook meat until no pink remains. Drain fat.
  3. Add in taco seasoning and follow packet directions.
  4. Stir in cilantro.
  5. Lay out tortillas and spread beans evenly in middle of each.
  6. Top each tortilla with equal parts meat and cheese, and roll up each tortilla burrito style.
  7. Heat non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add burrito seam side down and cook for 2 mins before flipping for another 2 mins
